Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 March 2026

1. Overview

Fuji Recipe Extractor reads Fujifilm JPEG metadata and shows recipe-related settings directly on your device. Privacy is built into the app design.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data.

We do not collect analytics, diagnostics, usage tracking, or advertising identifiers.

3. On-Device Processing

All image and metadata processing happens locally on your device.

Your photos and metadata do not leave your device through the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect data.

5. Accounts and Permissions

No account is required to use the app. You choose which images to open, and processing stays on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, because no personal data is collected by the app.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: fuji.recipe@jorgefrias.eu
